VR HAJJ SIMULATOUR

The VR Hajj Simulatour is a Final Year Project developed during my diploma studies. Using a VR headset, users can experience the Hajj rituals in an immersive, 3D animated environment. In my course where XR development isn't specifically taught, I need to independently learn and discover various aspects like creating 3D environments from scratch and integrating vital features in the VR application; this has able to increase my self-learn adaptability. Throughout this project, I have significantly improved my problem-solving, project management, and project documentation skills, as I serve as the project leader.

I plan to enhance the scope of this project, expanding its marketability as well as collaborating with industrialists that wish to revolutionise their systems. Lastly, my personal goals and aims would be to delve deeper into XR development, wishing that in the future I can start on experimenting with AR projects that incorporate human biometrics features.

This project is undergoing the procedure to obtain official patent and copyright protection through the Malaysian Intellectual Property Corporation (MyIPO).
